Robotics

I have some experience in designing and implementing robotic systems. Here are some of the robotic systems I have helped develop:

  • Real robotic arm operation: Use a 3D mouse to collect real robot data and deploy my own program.
  • ROS-based Intelligent autonomous car with SLAM mapping function: I designed and implemented an intelligent autonomous vehicle system based on ROS that can map specific areas and perform data collection.
  • Multi-sensor fusion vehicle system: Using qml，implemented the dashboard interface for the in-vehicle system, with relevant data intelligently collected through various sensors and displayed in real-time.
  • Composite unmanned vehicle control board (vehicle) and voice control relay control board (voice): Developed and designed PCB control boards using LCEDA , the former for driving our unmanned vehicle system, and the latter for controlling some functions of the intelligent vehicle system.
